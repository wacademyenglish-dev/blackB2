Track 04: The Origins

Too Personal

In 1992, "Black" became a massive radio hit. The record label demanded a music video to boost sales. Eddie Vedder flat out refused.

Why did he refuse?

Vedder said the song was about his first intense love. He felt that making a video would ruin the raw, personal meaning of the song and turn it into a commercial product.

Track 17: Structure

The Formula

The Imaginary Past IF + Past Perfect

(If I had known...)

The Imaginary Result WOULD HAVE + V3

(...I would have stayed.)

Track 19: Advanced

The Wish System

When we intensely regret a past action but don't need a "result" clause, we use:

WISH + Past Perfect

The Painful Reality

The Deep Regret

"I wish I had not been foolish."

Track 21: Pronunciation

Vocal Booth

Written (Formal) WOULD HAVE / COULD HAVE
Spoken (Native Reduction) WOULDA / COULDA

In emotional, fast speech, native speakers squish the grammar. "I would have stayed" becomes "I woulda stayed". Practice this fluidity!
